Start Here

New to cooking? No problem. This page is designed for beginners who want easy wins in the kitchen. Here you’ll find straightforward recipes, simple techniques, and tips for building your confidence.

Beginner Kitchen Tips

  • Choose recipes with fewer than 10 ingredients.
  • Read the entire recipe once before you start.
  • Prep your ingredients first: chop, measure, and gather everything.
  • Start with low or medium heat so food doesn’t burn quickly.
  • Taste as you cook and adjust seasoning slowly.

Essential Pantry Staples

Keeping a few basics at home makes it much easier to cook without planning every detail.

  • Pasta, rice, and oats
  • Olive oil or vegetable oil
  • Canned tomatoes and beans
  • Salt, pepper, garlic, and dried herbs
  • Eggs, yogurt, and frozen vegetables

Three Easy Starter Recipes

  1. One-Pan Veggie Pasta – pasta cooked with tomatoes, garlic, and any vegetables you like.
  2. 10-Minute Scrambled Eggs – soft eggs with butter, salt, and a bit of cheese.
  3. Basic Fried Rice – leftover rice stir-fried with veggies, soy sauce, and egg.

You can later add full instructions for each of these recipes on separate pages.
