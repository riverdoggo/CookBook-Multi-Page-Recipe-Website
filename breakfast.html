Breakfast Recipes

Start your morning with quick and satisfying meals. From sweet to savory, these recipes are simple enough for busy weekdays but delicious enough to enjoy any time.

Light, fluffy pancakes made with oats, ripe bananas, and a touch of cinnamon. Perfect with honey or maple syrup.

Avocado Toast Bowl

Creamy avocado spread over toasted bread, topped with cherry tomatoes, olive oil, and a squeeze of lemon.

Time: 10 min · Difficulty: Easy

Yogurt Berry Parfait

Layers of yogurt, granola, and fresh berries for a quick breakfast that feels like dessert.
